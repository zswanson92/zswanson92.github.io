About me

I'm a former lab rat turned Software/Data Engineer with experience using Python, Javascript, React, Pandas, and more. My most recent project is a Yelp! clone which was built using a React frontend and a Flask/SQLAlchemy backend that has create, read, update, and delete functionality for businesses, reviews and review responses.

In my free time I love going out to new restaurants and experiencing new food with my lovely wife Rene and son Herkules. I am a big fan of TV, movies and music of all genres. In my free time when not working on a side project, I also enjoy gaming, my go-tos being League of Legends & World of Warcraft Classic.

Having a background in natural sciences, I've always had a natural attraction to have a general concept or knowledge of "how things work". With the tech oriented world we live in and continue to envelop ourselves in, software/data engineering seemed like not only a great avenue for me to continue to understanding of how things around me work, but to engage my passion for logic and problem solving as well. I enjoy an environment that is dynamic, adaptive and never settles, and that is exactly what tech is.
